Recipe #1

Grilled Chicken Kabobs

  • Time: 35 minutes
  • Serves: 4-6 servings
Ingredients Quantity
Boneless skinless chicken breasts (or boneless thighs): x4
Oil x1 tbsp
Kosher Salt x1 tsp
Any Seasoning (Used is an all-purpose blend of smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt and black pepper.) x1/2 tbsp for each.
Skewer (Metal or Wooden) x1
Grill x1

Step 1: Brine the chicken
Soak the (whole) chicken breasts in a mixture of lukewarm water and salt for at least 15 minutes. Then rinse the chicken off with some cold water, pat the breasts dry with paper towels, and cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces.

Step 2: Season the chicken
Drizzle the chicken bites with olive oil, sprinkle with your desired seasonings, and toss well until the chicken is evenly coated.

Step 3: Prep the grill and skewers
Heat your grill to medium-high heat. Thread the chicken evenly onto your skewers.

Step 4: Grill
Then grill, cook the chicken for about 10-14 minutes, turning once halfway through, until the chicken is golden brown and cooked through. (Definitely avoid undercooking the chicken, but try to avoid overcooking as well so that it doesn’t dry out!)

Step 5: Serve warm
Transfer the kabobs to a clean plate, and loosely tent with aluminum foil for about 5 minutes. Then serve while they’re still nice and warm and juicy!

Recipe #2

Chicken Quesadillas

  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 10 servings
Ingredients Quantity
Skinless, boneless chicken breast, diced 1 lb
Packet fajita seasoning x1 (1.27 oz)
Vegetable oil 1 tbsp
Green bell peppers, chopped x2
red bell peppers, chopped x2
Onion, chopped x1
Flour tortillas 10 inches
Package shredded Cheddar cheese x1 (8 oz)
Package Shredded Monterey Jack Cheese x1 (8 oz)
Bacon Bits 1 tbsp

Step 1:
Gather ingredients, preheat the broiler, and lightly grease a baking sheet.

Step 2:
Toss chicken with fajita seasoning, then spread onto the prepared baking sheet. Place under the broiler and cook until chicken is cooked through and no longer pink in the center, about 5 minutes.

Step 3:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

Step 4:
Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Stir in bell peppers, onion, and broiled chicken. Cook and stir until vegetables have softened, about 10 minutes.

Step 5:
Layer half of each tortilla with chicken and vegetable mixture, Cheddar cheese, Monterey Jack cheese, and bacon bits.

Step 6:
Fold tortillas in half and place onto a baking sheet.

Step 7:
Bake quesadillas in the preheated oven until cheeses have melted, about 10 minutes. Cut each quesadilla into wedges.

Step 8:
Serve with sides of salsa and sour cream. Enjoy!

Source: https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/21659/chicken-quesadillas
